### Forms of Organization
Sole Proprietorship (some guy in a garage)
 - no legal protection (proprietor and the company are the same entity)
 - taxed the same as a person would be
 - easy to set up
Partnership (more than one guy in the garage)
 - same as above but with more than one owner
